SUMMARY

The total number of international tourist arrivals to Sri Lanka during August 2019 was 143,587. In comparison to

August last year, there was a decline of 28.3% when the arrivals were 200,359.

This month, the largest source market for tourists was India, followed by the United Kingdom and China.

Almost 99 % of tourists travelled by air to Sri Lanka.

As at 31st August 2019, 1,267,737 of tourists had visited Sri Lanka for this year. It is a decline of 19.9% over last

year when 1,582,835 of tourists has visited the country during the same period.
