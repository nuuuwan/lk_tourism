SUMMARY The total number of international tourist arrivals to Sri Lanka during November 2018 was 195,582. In comparison to November last year, there is a growth of 16.8% when the arrivals were 167,511.

This month, the largest source market for tourists is India, followed by United Kingdom and China.

Almost 94% of tourists travelled by air to Sri Lanka.

As at 30th November 2018, 2,080,627 tourists had visited Sri Lanka for this year. It is a 11.2% growth over last year when 1,871,871 tourists had visited the country during the same period.
